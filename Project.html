Srilanka

Known as The Pearl of the Indian Occen, Sri Lanka is a country off the southern cost of the Indian subcontinent in South Asia. It is home to glorious golden beaches, breathtaking landcaps & relices of ancient dynastics & civilization, and lays claim to a long colorful history of over three thousand years. Sri Lanka boasts is divers range of cultures, languages & relegions, and the combination of cultures has made it a hub for variety of foods, relegions &n arts. It is also blessed with abundant natural beauty including ten UNESCO World heritage sites,and one of the 25 biodeversity hotspots in the world
